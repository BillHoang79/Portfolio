Let me introduce myself

I was born and raised in Oakland, CA. I was always curious about how things worked and why they worked. One of my favorite things to do is to take apart things and figure out all the components and put them back together. Besides that I like to code and play games.

I've been coding on and off for the past 8 years. I started learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript all one my own 2 years ago. I went to sites like Udemy, codeacademy to study what I can understand, while working fulltime and trying to support myself. While learning all on my own I found it hard at times with out a mentor or anybody to help me out. I started to look for bootcamps, that is how I ended up at Codify Academy.
